Peplos Occasionwear

Dresses for Any Occasion

Peplos is a fashion brand with a twist. Our mission is to offer a stunning range of occasion and evening dresses sustainably. With each collection, we will allow a time window for you to purchase the dress of your dreams; the orders will then go to our trusted suppliers, who will produce the garment and ship it straight to your doorstep. This will ensure that we only produce what customers are already committed to buy and avoid shipping to a warehouse, thus helping reducing our carbon footprint.
